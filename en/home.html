Our Team

Broad expertise, tailored to your needs.

At VSC, our clients are our priority. We are committed to using our specialist knowledge and expertise to achieve excellent results. It’s our people that make all the difference, which is why we take the time to recruit candidates who have the right attitude: we focus on identifying individuals who can help us provide great service, communicate effectively and deliver on our promises.

We are a proud member of the Finnish-Turkish Business Association. We share the values of this historical association and support its goal of developing commercial cooperation between the Republics of Turkey and Finland.

We work in cooperation with Lakiasiaintoimisto SummanenLAW to guarantee our clients excellent legal assistance.

Daniel A. Vahtera

Managing Partner, CEO
With over eight years’ experience in the legal sector, Daniel speaks multiple languages including Finnish, English, Turkish and German. He specialises in foreign and business law and financial disputes and is experienced in working with international clients and organisations. As well as being the CEO of VSC Oy, he is also the Chairman of the Finnish-Turkish Business Association.

Rami Cherif

Operations Manager
Rami is the third founding partner of VSC Oy and is responsible for the operational side of our business, including accounts, invoicing and compliance. He has over five years’ experience in accounting and sales and speaks Finnish, English and Arabic.

Teemu T. Summanen

OTM, lawyer
Summanen focuses on criminal and foreigner cases, both in matters concerning residence permits and asylum. He has extensive personal expertise, especially in estates and other family law problems.
